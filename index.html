Forest Fires - SDS 322E Final Project

Ela Albiston, Wenting Lu, Anna Pham, Diego Fernandez, Theresa Pham, Yihan Du, Jennifer Amador-Gonzalez

Introduction

Forest fires are creating economical and ecological damage while endangering human lives. It is imperative to detect forest fires early in order to mitigate this issue. We want to make predictions on the forest fires in order to control them. If we are able to predict forest fires in advance, there will be greater preparation and resources for management.
